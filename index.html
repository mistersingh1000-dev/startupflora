Our Foundation

Education rooted in values and ready for tomorrow

Dahanukar English Medium School (DEMS) is located 4 km from Shrirampur town, between Rahata and Shrirampur blocks. The school serves children from urban and rural backgrounds with attention to mental, physical, moral, cultural and spiritual development.

  • Founded in 1978 by Mr. M.L. Dahanukar, fondly known as Babasaheb.
  • Led today by Mr. Amit Dahanukar and Mrs. Shivani Dahanukar.
  • Open admission policy irrespective of caste and creed.
  • Pre-primary, primary and secondary sections in one nurturing campus.

Academic Excellence at DEMS

Learning that builds confidence, creativity and character

At DEMS, education goes beyond textbooks. Structured academics are supported by experiential methods that strengthen understanding, discipline, innovation and holistic development.

  • Activity and project-based learning for practical understanding.
  • Play-way drills for joyful early education.
  • Research attitude that encourages curiosity and questioning.
  • Readers, weeklies and Book Bee initiatives for reading enhancement.
