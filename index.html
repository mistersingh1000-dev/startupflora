Dahanukar English Medium School (DEMS) is located 4 km from Shrirampur town, between Rahata and Shrirampur blocks. The school offers children a liberal education with attention to mental, physical, moral, cultural and spiritual development.

DEMS believes in activity and project-based learning, play-way drills, research attitude and co-curricular activities that broaden each child's experiences and skills.

Our teachers use research, creative problem solving and practical tools to help students understand concepts deeply and develop confidence, curiosity and problem-solving skills.

DEMS was founded in 1978 by Mr. M.L. Dahanukar, fondly known as Babasaheb, with the goal of bringing quality English-medium education to this region. Today it is led by Mr. Amit Dahanukar and Mrs. Shivani Dahanukar.
About DEMS

Education rooted in values and ready for tomorrow

The 22-acre campus has ample open play space and three sections: pre-primary, primary and secondary, with 24 classrooms. Admission is open to all, irrespective of caste and creed.
